About Us

Studsat club was started by a group of engineering students studying at Nitte Menakashi institute of Technology in the year 2010 july,students and teachers designed, developed and fabricated India’s first Pico – Satellite (weighing 850 gm) with the guidance of ISRO. The Satellite, named as STUDSAT, took to the orbit in July 2010 from Sriharikota aboard the PSLVC – 15 with CARTOSAT 2B and was placed into the orbit of 630km above the earth. and was successfully tracked by the Ground Station NASTRAC at NMIT.
